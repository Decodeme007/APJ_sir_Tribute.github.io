A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India


Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam is a famous name in the whole world.
He is counted among the greatest scientists of the 21st century. Even more, he becomes the 11th president of India and served his country. He was the most valued person of the country as his contribution as a scientist and as a president is beyond compare. Apart from that, his contribution to the ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization) is remarkable. He headed many projects that contributed to the society also he was the one who helped in the development of Agni and Prithvi missiles. For his involvement in the Nuclear power in India, he was known as “Missile Man of India”. And due to his contribution to the country, the government awarded him with the highest civilian award.

Biography

  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam is also known as Missile Man.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was born in the Dhanushkodi village of Rameswaram which is now located in Tamil Nadu.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam belonged to a poor family and used to sell newspapers to complete his studies.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was also the President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam died on 27 July 2015 during a lecture in Shillong Meghalaya.
